              *italic* **bold** ***bold italic***

              Headings

              # section heading ## sub-section heading ### sub-sub-section heading

              Bullet points

              * first point * second point * third point

              Numbered lists

              1. first point 2. second point 3. third point

              Indented lists

              * top level bullet one   * sub-bullet   * sub-bullet 2 * top level bullet two 1. numbered point one   1. nested numbered point 2. numbered point two
